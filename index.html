About

Building useful AI systems.

I am a graduate student in Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego. My interests sit at the intersection of language models, agentic reasoning, retrieval systems, and real-world machine learning deployment.

I like work that connects research ideas to systems that people can actually use. That includes model behavior, evaluation, tool use, infrastructure, and the details that make an ML product reliable instead of brittle.

My recent experience spans LLM-powered storytelling systems at Tencent AI Lab, production web and AI systems at Ai4C, and current research on agentic workflows for weather science. Across all of these, I care about grounded generation, efficient execution, and building interfaces that help complex models do something genuinely useful.

I am especially interested in roles and collaborations around LLM systems, scientific AI, multimodal learning, and applied machine learning engineering.
