About

Engineering useful AI systems, not just demos.

I am a graduate student at the University of California, San Diego, with a background in mathematics and computer science. I enjoy building end-to-end AI products: from model design and evaluation to tooling, deployment, and user-facing systems.

My experience includes LLM-powered game narration at Tencent AI Lab, production web and AI systems at Ai4C, and current research on agentic frameworks that help scientists reason over complex weather and climate data.

What I work on

  • LLM products that need grounded generation, low latency, and clear evaluation.
  • Agent systems that use tools, code execution, and iterative self-correction.
  • Applied ML infrastructure that can move from research ideas into production.
