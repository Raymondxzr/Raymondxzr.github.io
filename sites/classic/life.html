Outside work, I enjoy things that make me observe more carefully. I like traveling without too much of a plan, walking through new places, and noticing the architecture, light, streets, transit, and small details that shape a city’s character.

Photography grew out of that habit. I am drawn less to documenting everything and more to capturing atmosphere: afternoon light, quiet streets, reflections, and simple compositions that feel balanced.

Sports reset me in a different way. Badminton gives me speed and rhythm; tennis feels more patient and technical. Both are good counterweights to long stretches of reading, coding, and focused work.
